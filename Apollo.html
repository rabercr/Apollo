About Me

My name is Carly Raber. I was born and raised in Southern California and Central Oregon. I began learning about art at a very young age and my passion for it originated from my Grandma Kathy. When I was little, she taught me how to color within the lines, shade, paint and more. As I got older, I began to experiment with my artistic creativity. We started our own family buissness where we handmade decorative yard signs, created seasonal crafts, and painted themed bedrooms.

On this website you will find cutomized shoes, clothing, electronics, and accessories that are meant to last for years to come. I hope you find as much joy in using them as I have in creating them!
